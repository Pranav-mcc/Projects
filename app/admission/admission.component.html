Arena Academy Sports Shine Course



There are many deserving and persistent student-athletes who want to make their mark and gain 
world-class exposure in their field of sports. Most of these young athletes are not aware of 
how they can truly be an asset to the top world-class universities abroad. The Arena Academy 
in association with Arena Sports Academy is committed to creating an active and vibrant 
student community.Through Arena Sports Shine course,we offer a flexible educational curriculum
that provides a tailor-made academic schedule to athletes, built around their sporting commits.
We encourage & provide means to our students, from Grade 6 to Grade 12, to balance their pursuit
for sporting excellence without compromising on their academics. We create lifelong learners who
can balance sports as well as academics
